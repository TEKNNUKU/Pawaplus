Our Story

Pawa+9ja is Nigeria's most trusted solar marketplace. We're on a mission to bring sustainable, reliable, and affordable energy solutions to every home and business.

Our Mission

To empower Nigerians with clean and consistent energy, ending the reliance on generators and grid instability. We believe that everyone deserves access to uninterrupted power.

Our Values

  • Reliability: We only sell products that we trust, ensuring you get consistent, long-lasting power.
  • Affordability: With flexible payment options like 'Pay Small-Small', we make solar accessible to everyone.
  • Customer Trust: We build long-term relationships through transparent pricing, excellent service, and a swap program for old products.
  • Innovation: We stay ahead of the curve, offering the latest solar technology to meet your evolving needs.
